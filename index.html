ABOUT Me

I'm Rahul Hanumant Jadhav, currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from Zeal College of Engineering, Pune. My journey in tech started with a Diploma in Computer Engineering and has evolved through hands-on projects, internships, and a deep curiosity for solving problems with technology. My core skills include C++, Python, Django, SQL, and web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’ve also worked with cloud platforms like AWS and have a growing interest in data analytics and system design.
